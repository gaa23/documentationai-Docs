title: How do I invite my team?
description: Add teammates to your MyDose project.
---

## Overview

Invite teammates to collaborate on your MyDose documentation.

## Steps

1. Go to your project dashboard.
2. Click on "Team" in the sidebar.
3. Select "Invite Member".
4. Enter the email address of your teammate.
5. Click "Send Invitation".

Your teammate will receive an email with a join link.
