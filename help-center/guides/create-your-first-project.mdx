## Overview

Set up your first project in MyDose in minutes.

## Steps
<Steps>
  <Step title="Login">
    Sign in to your MyDose account at `https://mydose.com`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="New Project">
    Click "New Project" on your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name & Description">
    Fill in project name and optional description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create">
    Click "Create" to finish. Your workspace is now ready.
  </Step>
</Steps>
