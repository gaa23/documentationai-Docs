## When to create a custom plan

[Assigning a package](/clinic-guide/assign-package) is the fastest way to start a patient on treatment — it pre-fills everything from a template. Create a custom treatment plan when:

- No existing package matches the patient's protocol
- You need to modify dosage, titration, or duration beyond what a package allows
- You're prescribing a one-off or experimental protocol

## Before you start

Your prescriber credentials must be on file before you can finalize any treatment plan. If you haven't added them yet, see [Set Up Prescriber Credentials](/clinic-guide/prescriber-credentials).

## Creating a treatment plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Treatment Plans">
    In the sidebar, select **Treatment Plans**, then select **New**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the patient">
    Search for and select the patient this plan is for. The patient must already exist in your system. If not, [add them first](/clinic-guide/add-patient).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the medication">
    Select the medication or peptide from your clinic's inventory. This determines the available dosage units (mg, µg, IU, mL, Units).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define the treatment protocol">
    Set the dosage and schedule:

    - **Initial dosage** — the starting dose and unit
    - **Frequency** — how often the patient takes the dose (e.g., once weekly)
    - **Duration** — how long the initial phase runs
    - **Titration schedule** — add phases to escalate the dose over time. Each phase has its own dosage, frequency, and duration.
    - **Maintenance dosage** — the dose the patient stays on after titration completes
    - **Number of treatments** — total administrations across all phases
    - **Appointment link** — optionally link a scheduled consultation to this plan
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add prescriber information">
    Confirm or enter your credentials:

    - **NPI number** — National Provider Identifier
    - **DEA number** — Drug Enforcement Administration registration
    - **State medical license** — for the state where the patient is located

    These are saved to your profile and auto-filled on future plans.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Attach consent forms">
    Select any consent forms the patient must sign before treatment begins. You can attach multiple forms. If the required form doesn't exist yet, see [Create a Consent Form](/clinic-guide/consent-forms).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and create">
    Review the full plan — protocol, prescriber info, and consent requirements. Select **Create** to generate the treatment plan.

    The patient is notified and the plan appears in their portal under **Treatments** with a status of **Pending Patient**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens after creation

The patient must accept the plan before it becomes active. If the plan includes an intake questionnaire, they complete it after accepting. Once submitted, the intake appears in your [Action Center](/clinic-guide/review-intakes) for approval.

See [treatment plan statuses](/clinic-guide/assign-package#treatment-plan-statuses) for a full breakdown of each state.
