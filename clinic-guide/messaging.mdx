## Messaging overview

The messaging feature lets your clinic communicate directly with patients inside the platform. Patients can message your team from their portal, and your team can initiate or respond to conversations from the clinic side.

All messages are tied to a specific patient, keeping communication organized and accessible alongside their profile, treatment plan, and intake history.

## Reading and responding to messages

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Messaging">
    In the sidebar, select **Messaging**. Your inbox shows all active conversations, sorted by most recent activity. Unread messages are highlighted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a conversation">
    Select a patient's conversation to open the message thread. You can see the full history of messages exchanged with that patient.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reply">
    Type your message in the reply field and send. The patient receives a notification in their portal.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Starting a new conversation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Messaging">
    In the sidebar, select **Messaging**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a new message">
    Select **New Message** and search for the patient you want to contact.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write and send">
    Type your message and send. The conversation thread opens and the patient is notified.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Messaging from a patient's profile

You can also start or continue a conversation directly from a patient's profile. Open the patient's record and select the **Messages** tab to see their conversation history or send a new message.

## Tips for clinical messaging

- Use messaging for follow-up questions, appointment reminders, or clarifying intake responses — not for sharing prescription details or sensitive clinical decisions that require a formal record.
- Patients are notified when they receive a message but may not respond immediately. For urgent matters, follow up by phone.
- All messages are visible to any clinic team member with patient access, not just the sender.
