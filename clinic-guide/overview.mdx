## What is MyDose.ai?

MyDose.ai is a practice management platform built for clinics offering longevity therapy. It connects your clinical team with patients through a single system that handles patient onboarding, treatment planning, appointment scheduling, pharmacy fulfillment, and compliance tracking.

Your team manages everything from the **Clinic Dashboard**. Patients interact through their own portal to book consultations, review treatment plans, log doses, and message your team.

## What your team can do

**Manage patients**\
Add patients, invite them to the portal, and view their full profile — contact info, active treatments, vitals history, and dose logs — in one place.

**Build and assign treatment packages**\
Create reusable treatment packages that define a medication, dosage, titration schedule, pricing, and consent requirements. Assign packages directly to patients to instantly generate a treatment plan.

**Schedule and run consultations**\
Manage your calendar, set provider availability, and define consultation types. Patients book from your available slots. Telehealth consultations include a built-in intake and questionnaire flow.

**Prescribe and track treatments**\
Create treatment plans with full prescriber credentials (NPI, DEA, state license). Track whether patients have accepted their plan, completed intake forms, and signed consent documents.

**Order through the pharmacy marketplace**\
Browse and order medications from integrated suppliers. Choose between marketplace fulfillment (supplier ships direct) or clinic fulfillment.

**Monitor patient progress**\
Review dose logs and vitals submitted by patients. See how patients are responding to treatment over time.

## How roles work

| Role                | What they can do                                                      |
| ------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Admin               | Full access to all features and settings                              |
| Prescriber / Doctor | Create treatment plans, approve intakes, access calendar and patients |
| Staff               | Manage patients and appointments; cannot prescribe                    |

Permissions are configured in **Settings → User Management**.

## Key terms

**Services&#xA0;**— A clinic-defined treatment template. Includes medication, dosage protocol, titration schedule, pricing, and consent forms. Packages are reusable across patients.

**Treatment Plan** — A patient-specific instance of a Service. Created when you assign a package to a patient or prescribe a custom plan. The patient must accept the plan before treatment begins.

**Intake** — A health questionnaire the patient completes before or after a package is assigned. Your team reviews and approves it before prescribing.

**Consultation Type** — A bookable appointment format (e.g., 30-min initial consult, 15-min follow-up) with its own duration and pricing.
