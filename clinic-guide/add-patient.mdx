## Adding a patient

When you add a patient, you create their profile in your clinic and optionally send them an invitation to access the patient portal. Patients cannot book appointments or view treatment plans until they accept their invite and complete their profile setup.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Patients list">
    In the sidebar, select **Patients**. This shows all current patients at your clinic along with their status, active treatments, and contact info.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a new patient">
    Select **Add Patient** in the top-right corner. Enter the patient's details:
    - First and last name
    - Email address
    - Phone number
    - Location / address

    Save the profile to create the patient record.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send a portal invitation">
    From the patient's profile, select **Send Invite**. This emails the patient a link to create their account and complete their profile setup.

    The patient appears as **Pending** in your list until they accept and finish onboarding. Patients must add a payment method and confirm their location before they can book consultations.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens next

Once a patient accepts their invitation and completes setup, they appear as active in your patient list. You can then:

- [Assign a package](/clinic-guide/assign-package) to start a treatment plan
- [Create a bookable appointment](/clinic-guide/setup-patient-booking) they can schedule themselves
- Create a custom treatment plan from **Treatment Plans → New**

## Importing multiple patients

To add patients in bulk, use the **Import** option on the Patients page. Upload a CSV with the required fields. Imported patients still need to be invited individually to gain portal access.
