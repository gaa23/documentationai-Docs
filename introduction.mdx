## Overview

MyDose serves as the operating system for protocol-based longevity care. You manage integrative, regenerative, and functional medicine workflows in one unified platform. It connects clinics, patients, telehealth services, and AI tools to support long-term treatment plans rather than isolated visits.

The platform handles provider-managed therapies, lab results, and biomarker tracking. Clinics gain structured protocols, patients receive clear progress views, and partners access context-aware AI insights. Start building personalized longevity programs today.
